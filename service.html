Food & Nutrition

Food and nutrition are essential for maintaining good health, growth, and energy. Eating a balanced diet ensures the body gets all the nutrients it needs to function properly and helps prevent malnutrition and lifestyle-related diseases.

Key Practices:

  • Balanced Diet: Eat a variety of foods ‚Äî carbohydrates, proteins, fruits, and vegetables ‚Äî in the right amounts.
  • Safe Food Handling: Wash fruits and vegetables before eating, cook food thoroughly, and store leftovers safely.
  • Regular Meals: Eat breakfast, lunch, and dinner at consistent times to maintain energy levels.
  • Hydration: Drink enough clean water daily to stay hydrated and support body functions.
  • Limit Junk Food: Reduce intake of sugary drinks, fried snacks, and processed foods.

Benefits of Good Nutrition:

  • Strengthens the immune system and reduces risk of infections.
  • Supports proper growth and development in children.
  • Improves focus, memory, and overall mental performance.
  • Helps maintain healthy weight and prevents lifestyle diseases.
