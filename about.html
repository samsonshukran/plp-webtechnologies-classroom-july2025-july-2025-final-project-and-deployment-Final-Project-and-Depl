About Health Hub

Health Reminder is a simple and helpful web app designed to keep you observant about your health every day.

Our mission is to help people stay healthy by providing:

  • Reminders for daily healthy habits
  • Tips on nutrition, exercise, and hygiene
  • Updates on health awareness campaigns
  • Encouragement to go for regular checkups

This website was built to encourage a healthy lifestyle and make health monitoring easier for everyone.
